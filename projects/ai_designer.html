SUMMARY

AI-Designer is a novel data-driven tool for graphic design.
It provides a way to:
 • Generate high-quality sketch drawings by learn- ing the sequences of strokes.
 • Support drawing sketches of characters with emotional expressions based on input strokes from the user.
 • Generate an advertising poster based on a user-specified product image and its corresponding text descriptions.
Keywords: Design Support Tools, Data-Driven Design, Machine Learning
